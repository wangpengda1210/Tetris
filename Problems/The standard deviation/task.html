The standard deviation

You decided to choose a birthday present for your friend. Let's say you chose a book about wild animals. You looked it up in five online-shops and wrote down the prices of the book. There are five integers in the input, one per line. Use all of them to make a list (dataset), then print the rounded standard deviation of the prices. To round it, use the function round().


Sample Input:
35
5
23
6
10

Sample Output:
12
